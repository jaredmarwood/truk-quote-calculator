## Key Messaging Principles

1. **Lead with the problem they know** — spreadsheets, paper quotes, guesswork on fuel costs
2. **No jargon, no hype** — speak like a tradie, not a startup
3. **Low commitment** — "5 minutes, no signup" not "join our beta program"
4. **Real benefit for them** — free access, shape the product, save time
5. **No hard sell** — they're helping us build, not buying anything

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't mention "platform", "SaaS", "disrupt", or "AI"
- Don't lead with features — lead with the problem
- Don't make it sound like a survey — make it sound like a tool
- Don't over-promise "real-time" if alpha has hardcoded prices
