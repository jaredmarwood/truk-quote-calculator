# Alpha Testing Target List — Quote Calculator

**Date:** 2026-07-15
**Owner:** Tye (Marketing)
**Purpose:** Identify 5+ real Australian owner-operators or small fleets (5-20 trucks) using spreadsheets for quoting, for alpha quote calculator testing.

## Target Profile
- **Size:** Owner-operator or small fleet (1-20 trucks)
- **Current method:** Manual spreadsheets or paper-based quoting
- **Industry:** Trucking (tipper, prime mover, heavy haulage) or earthmoving (bobcat, excavator, dozer)
- **Location:** Australia (all states)
- **Decision maker:** Owns the business, makes quoting decisions

## Outreach Channels

### 1. Australian Trucking Association (ATA)
- **Website:** https://www.truck.net.au
- **Email:** info@truck.net.au
- **Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/truck.australia (22,454 likes)
- **Note:** The ATA represents owner-drivers and small fleets. Contact for member outreach or ask if they'll share our testing invitation.

### 2. Earthworks Hub Business Directory
- **URL:** https://earthworkshub.com.au/directory
- **Note:** Directory of earthmoving and civil construction contractors across Australia. Browse for small contractors to reach out to directly.

### 3. SMEA Excavating & Earth Moving Contractors Directory
- **URL:** https://www.smea.org.au/australia/excavating-20-earth-moving-contractors
- **Note:** Directory of excavating contractors. Good source for small owner-operator businesses.

### 4. Facebook Groups (High Engagement)

- **Excavator Operators Australia and NZ:** https://www.facebook.com/groups/excavator.operators.australia

- **Truck and Plant Operators Australia:** https://www.facebook.com/groups/242496606739875

### 5. SEEK Owner Operator Listings
- **URL:** https://au.seek.com/owner-operator-jobs
- **Note:** While this is a job board, operators posting or responding to these listings are actively engaged in owner-operator work. We could also look at companies hiring owner operators as potential customers (they're our profile).

### 6. Australian Trucking Association Regional Groups
- Check state-level trucking associations:
  - Transport Council of NSW
  - Master Truckers Association of WA
  - Australian Road Transport Association (Vic)
  - Queensland Trucking Association

## Contact Strategy

### Approach: Direct outreach via email or Facebook
1. **Initial contact:** Send a concise message explaining Truk, the problem we solve (fuel cost accuracy in quoting), and invite them to test our new quote calculator.
2. **Value prop for them:** Free access during beta, shape the product to their needs, early adopter pricing.
3. **Low friction:** No signup required for the alpha test — just open the calculator and enter a route.
