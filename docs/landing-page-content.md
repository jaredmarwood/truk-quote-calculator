## HOW IT WORKS (3 STEPS)

**1. Enter your route**

Type in the distance, pick your equipment, and set your fuel consumption. Addresses auto-complete and routes cache for when the signal drops.

**2. Set your margin**

Your margin. Not a hardcoded number. See base cost and sell price side by side so you know exactly what you're making on every job.

**3. Get your quote**

Turn the calculation into a professional branded PDF quote in seconds. Email it to your customer. They accept or decline with one tap — no login, no signup.

## FEATURES

### Know your true costs

Stop guessing what a job costs. Truk calculates your real per-km and per-hour costs using your actual running expenses.

- **Live diesel prices** — pulls current prices from government fuel APIs across every Australian state. Your quotes always reflect today's fuel cost, not last week's.
- **Address autocomplete** — type a few letters, pick the address. Works fast, even on a mobile connection.
- **Profit margin control** — set your own margin and see base cost vs sell price instantly. Adjust on the fly.
- **Fleet comparison** — own multiple trucks? See side-by-side pricing to find the cheapest option for each job.
- **Advanced extras** — empty running, tolls, permits, escorts, downtime delays. Nothing gets missed.

### Quote with confidence

Turn a cost calculation into a professional branded quote in seconds. Email it to your customer.

- **Multi-equipment quotes** — quoting a dozer and an excavator for the same job? Separate calculations, one total.
- **Branded PDFs** — your logo, ABN, business details on every quote. Looks professional without a designer.
- **Customer accept/decline** — your customer gets a link. One tap to accept or decline. No login, no signup, no friction.
- **Auto-expiry + reminders** — quotes expire automatically. Resend with one click.
- **Revision history** — every edit is tracked. Revise a sent quote without losing the original.

### Run your fleet in one place

Truk isn't just for trucks. It's for every piece of kit on your job.

- **Truck + machinery inventory** — add, edit, archive. See per-km and per-hour costs instantly.
- **Trailer pairings** — link trailers to trucks with default combinations.
- **New quote from any truck** — one click from the fleet list opens a pre-filled calculator.
- **Team access** — invite drivers and admins. Control who sees what. No passwords to remember.
