Thanks for agreeing to test our quote calculator. We really appreciate you taking the time — this kind of feedback is exactly what we need.

**Here's what we'll do in our 30 minutes:**

- **First 5 min:** I'll introduce myself, explain how the session works

**Before our session, if you have time:**

Open the calculator at **[truk.au/calculator](https://truk.au/calculator)** on your phone and have a play. Think about:
- Does the fuel cost it gives you look right?
- Is it faster than what you currently do (spreadsheet/paper/whatever)?
- What would you change?

No pressure if you haven't tried it yet — we'll go through it together during the session.
