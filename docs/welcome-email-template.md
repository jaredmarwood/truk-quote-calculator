## If You Haven't Tried It Yet

No worries — you can jump straight into the calculator yourself:

👉 **[truk.au/calculator](https://truk.au/calculator)**

Enter a real job you're quoting (distance, fuel consumption, diesel price — whatever you've got) and see what the calculator spits out. No signup needed.

If the numbers look right to you, there'll be a "Leave Feedback" link on the results page. Click it and tell us what you think — takes about 5 minutes.

That's how most operators find us now — we don't have a dedicated recruitment list anymore, just the calculator sitting out there for anyone who needs it.

## What We're Looking For

- **Accuracy confidence:** Does the fuel cost calculation look right to you?
- **Usability:** Was it easy to enter your job details?
- **Missing pieces:** Any fields or features you wish it had?
- **Willingness to pay:** If this saved you time and guesswork, what would a fair monthly price be?
